## Hugo

[Hugo](https://github.com/gohugoio/hugo) is a fast and modern static site generator written in Go.

- `hugo new posts/your-post-name.md`: Creates a new article with automatically added YAML front matter.
- `hugo server -D`: Runs the site locally.
